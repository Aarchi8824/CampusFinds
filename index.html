How it works

Four simple steps to get reunited with your belongings

1

Lost or found?

Spotted something on campus or missing your belongings?

2

Submit a report

Fill a quick form with a photo, category and location. Takes under a minute.

3

Browse the gallery

Search by category or date to find your item quickly.

4

Get reunited

Contact the finder directly using the details in the report.

No account or login needed. Open to all students via a shared link. Built using free and open source tools — Baserow for the database and GitHub Pages for hosting.
